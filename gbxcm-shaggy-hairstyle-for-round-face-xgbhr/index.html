Shaggy Hairstyle For Round Face: A Guide For 2023

Posted on April 3, 2023 by admin

Introduction

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want something that will flatter your face shape and bring out your best features. One option that has been popular for years is the shaggy hairstyle. In this article, we will explore how to rock a shaggy hairstyle with a round face in 2023.

What is a Shaggy Hairstyle?

A shaggy hairstyle is a layered haircut that features choppy ends and lots of texture. It can be cut to any length, from a short pixie cut to long layers. The key to a shaggy hairstyle is the layers, which add movement and volume to the hair.

Why Shaggy Hairstyle is Perfect for Round Faces?

A shaggy hairstyle is perfect for round faces because it adds height and volume to the crown of the head, elongating the face. The layers also help to frame the face and draw attention to the cheekbones and jawline. Additionally, the choppy ends add texture and movement to the hair, giving the illusion of a slimmer face.

How to Style a Shaggy Hairstyle for Round Face?

To style a shaggy hairstyle for a round face, start by applying a volumizing mousse to damp hair. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, lifting at the roots to add volume. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add more texture and movement to the hair. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to add waves or curls to your shaggy hairstyle. This will give your hair more body and movement, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

Best Shaggy Hairstyles for Round Faces in 2023

There are many different shaggy hairstyles that are perfect for round faces in 2023. Here are some of the best options: 1. Short Shaggy Pixie Cut – This is a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. The short layers add texture and volume to the hair, while the pixie cut elongates the face. 2. Shoulder-Length Shaggy Bob – This is a great option for those who want a longer hairstyle. The layers add movement and texture to the hair, while the bob shape elongates the face. 3. Long Shaggy Layers – This is a great option for those who want a longer hairstyle with lots of movement. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, while the length elongates the face.

How to Choose the Right Shaggy Hairstyle for Your Round Face?

When choosing a shaggy hairstyle for your round face, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style. If you have thin hair, a shorter shaggy hairstyle may be best to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer shaggy hairstyle with lots of layers may be best to add movement and texture. Consider your lifestyle as well. If you have a busy schedule and don’t have a lot of time to style your hair, a shorter shaggy hairstyle may be best. If you have more time to style your hair, a longer shaggy hairstyle with lots of layers may be a great option. Lastly, consider your personal style. If you prefer a more edgy look, a shorter shaggy hairstyle with choppy layers may be best. If you prefer a more natural look, a longer shaggy hairstyle with softer layers may be a great option.

Care and Maintenance of Shaggy Hairstyle for Round Face

To maintain your shaggy hairstyle for a round face, you should visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim. This will help to keep the layers looking fresh and prevent split ends. You should also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and make it look limp and lifeless.

Conclusion

In conclusion, a shaggy hairstyle is a great option for those with round faces in 2023. It adds height and volume to the crown of the head, elongating the face and framing the cheekbones and jawline. With the right styling and maintenance, you can rock a shaggy hairstyle that flatters your face shape and brings out your best features.
