Spiral Perm Short Hair: Tips And Tricks For The Perfect Look In 2023

Posted on January 10, 2023 by admin

Introduction

Short hair can be both practical and stylish, but it can also feel limiting when it comes to styling options. That’s where spiral perms come in – a chemical treatment that creates beautiful, bouncy curls that last for months. In this article, we’ll share tips and tricks for getting the perfect spiral perm on short hair in 2023.

What is a spiral perm?

A spiral perm is a type of permanent wave that creates a spiral pattern in the hair. It’s achieved by wrapping small sections of hair around perm rods, which are then treated with a chemical solution to break down the hair’s natural bonds and reshape it into a curly pattern. The result is beautiful, bouncy curls that can last for up to six months.

Choosing the Right Perm Rods

Choosing the right perm rods is crucial for achieving the perfect spiral perm on short hair. For short hair, you’ll want to use smaller perm rods to create tighter curls. We recommend using ⅜ inch or ½ inch perm rods to achieve the perfect spiral curl. Additionally, consider the length of your hair when selecting perm rods – shorter hair will require shorter perm rods to achieve the best results.

Preparing Your Hair for a Spiral Perm

Before getting a spiral perm, it’s important to prepare your hair for the chemical treatment. This means avoiding shampooing your hair for 24-48 hours before your appointment, as well as avoiding any other chemical treatments like hair dye or relaxers. Additionally, be sure to deep condition your hair a few days before your appointment to ensure it’s well-moisturized and healthy.

The Spiral Perm Process

When you arrive at the salon for your spiral perm, your stylist will start by washing and conditioning your hair. They’ll then section your hair and apply the perm solution to each section, wrapping it around the selected perm rods. After the perm solution has set, your stylist will rinse your hair and apply a neutralizer to set the curls. After a final rinse and blow-dry, you’ll have beautiful, bouncy spiral curls!

Styling Your Spiral Perm

Styling your spiral perm is easy and versatile. You can wear your curls down and natural, or you can style them into an updo for a more formal look. To keep your curls looking their best, use a curl-enhancing product like mousse or serum and avoid using heated styling tools like flat irons or curling wands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does a spiral perm last?

A: A spiral perm can last for up to six months, depending on your hair’s natural growth rate and how well you care for your curls.

Q: Can I get a spiral perm if my hair is already colored?

A: Yes, but it’s important to wait at least two weeks after coloring your hair before getting a spiral perm to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I get a spiral perm if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, but keep in mind that the tighter the curl, the more damage the perm can cause to your hair. Talk to your stylist about the best approach for your hair type.

Q: Can I wash my hair after getting a spiral perm?

A: Yes, but it’s important to wait at least 48 hours after getting a spiral perm to avoid disrupting the curl pattern. After that, you can wash your hair as usual, but be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that’s designed for curly hair to keep your curls looking their best.
