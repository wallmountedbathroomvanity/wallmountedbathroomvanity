Cute Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair In 2023

Posted on October 8, 2022 by admin

Introduction

Shoulder length hair is one of the most versatile hair lengths out there. It’s long enough to style in many ways, but short enough to manage easily. If you’re looking for some cute hairstyles for your shoulder length hair, you’re in luck because we’ve got you covered.

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray to your damp hair. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create a messy look. Finish off with some hairspray to hold everything in place.

2. Braided Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still want to show off their length. Start by parting your hair in the middle. Then, take a section of hair from each side of your head and braid it. Bring the braids to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.

3. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that looks effortlessly chic. To do this hairstyle, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

4. Sleek and Straight

For a more polished look, try straightening your shoulder length hair. Apply a heat protectant to your hair and use a straightening iron to straighten your hair. Finish off with some hair serum to add shine and combat frizz.

5. Top Knot

The top knot is a trendy hairstyle that’s perfect for those lazy days. To do this hairstyle, start by pulling your hair up into a high ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

6. Side Swept Bangs

If you have shoulder length hair with bangs, try sweeping them to the side for a new look. Apply some volumizing mousse to your hair and use a round brush to blow dry your hair. Then, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs and sweep them to the side.

7. Half-Up Bun

The half-up bun is a trendy and cute hairstyle that’s perfect for shoulder length hair. Start by taking a section of hair from the crown of your head and pulling it back. Twist the section and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

8. Low Ponytail

The low ponytail is a classic hairstyle that’s perfect for any occasion. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Then, use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair. Finish off with some hairspray to hold the curls in place.

9. Twisted Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is a twist on the classic half-up half-down style. Start by taking a section of hair from each side of your head and twisting them. Bring the twists to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.

10. Double Dutch Braids

Double Dutch braids are a cute and trendy hairstyle that’s perfect for shoulder length hair. Start by parting your hair in the middle. Then, take a section of hair from each side of your head and braid them. Secure the braids with elastic bands and pancake them to make them look fuller.

Conclusion

With these cute hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you’ll never have a bad hair day again. Try one of these hairstyles and switch up your look for 2023.

Questions and Answers

1. Can I achieve beachy waves without a curling iron?

Yes, you can achieve beachy waves without a curling iron by braiding your damp hair and letting it air dry. Once it’s dry, undo the braids and scrunch your hair with your hands.

2. How can I prevent frizz when straightening my hair?

To prevent frizz when straightening your hair, use a heat protectant and a straightening iron with ceramic plates. Finish off with some hair serum to add shine and combat frizz.

3. Can I wear a half-up bun with short bangs?

Yes, you can wear a half-up bun with short bangs. Instead of pulling back a section of hair from the crown of your head, pull back a section of hair from the sides of your head.

4. How do I pancake my braids?

To pancake your braids, gently pull on the sides of the braid to make it look fuller. This will create a more voluminous and trendy look.
