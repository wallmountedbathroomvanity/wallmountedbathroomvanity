10 Trendy Short Hairstyles With Shaved Sides And Back In 2023

Posted on July 25, 2022 by admin

Introduction

Short hairstyles with shaved sides and back have been in trend for many years, and they are still going strong in 2023. This hairstyle is edgy and trendy, and it can be customized to suit your personality and style. Shaved sides and back add an extra dimension to the hairstyle, making it stand out from the crowd. Here are ten trendy short hairstyles with shaved sides and back that you can try out in 2023.

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that has been in fashion for decades. It is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. In 2023, the pixie cut takes a modern twist with shaved sides and back. The shaved sides and back give the pixie cut an edgy and trendy look that is perfect for the younger generation.

How do I style a pixie cut with shaved sides and back?

To style a pixie cut with shaved sides and back, use a pomade or wax to add texture to your hair. Apply the product to your hair while it is still damp and style it with your fingers. You can create a messy look or a sleek and sophisticated look, depending on your preference.

The Bob

The bob is another classic hairstyle that has been popular for many years. In 2023, the bob takes a daring twist with shaved sides and back. The shaved sides and back give the bob a unique and edgy look that is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

What is the best length for a bob with shaved sides and back?

The best length for a bob with shaved sides and back is chin-length or shorter. This length will enhance the edginess of the hairstyle and make it stand out.

The Mohawk

The Mohawk is a bold and daring hairstyle that has been popular among the punk rock community for many years. In 2023, the Mohawk takes a modern twist with shaved sides and back. The shaved sides and back create a contrast with the longer hair on top, making the hairstyle stand out even more.

Do I need to have long hair to create a Mohawk with shaved sides and back?

No, you do not need to have long hair to create a Mohawk with shaved sides and back. You can create a faux Mohawk by styling the longer hair on top of your head and shaving the sides and back.

The Undercut

The undercut is a popular hairstyle that has been in trend for many years. In 2023, the undercut takes a daring twist with shaved sides and back. The shaved sides and back create a unique and edgy look that is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

Can I style my undercut with shaved sides and back in different ways?

Yes, you can style your undercut with shaved sides and back in different ways. You can add texture to your hair, create a messy look, or slick it back for a more sophisticated look.

The Shag

The shag is a popular hairstyle that has been in trend for many years. In 2023, the shag takes a daring twist with shaved sides and back. The shaved sides and back create an edgy and unique look that is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

What is the best way to style a shag with shaved sides and back?

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a popular hairstyle that has been in trend for many years. In 2023, the buzz cut takes a daring twist with shaved sides and back. The shaved sides and back create an edgy and unique look that is perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd.

Can I add some texture to my buzz cut with shaved sides and back?

The Afro

The Bowl Cut

What is the best way to style a bowl cut with shaved sides and back?

Conclusion

Short hairstyles with shaved sides and back are edgy and trendy, and they are still going strong in 2023. These hairstyles are perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd and make a bold statement. Try out these ten trendy short hairstyles with shaved sides and back and see which one suits your personality and style.

FAQs

1. Will shaving the sides and back of my hair damage it?

No, shaving the sides and back of your hair will not damage it. However, make sure that you go to a professional hairstylist who knows how to shave hair properly to avoid any accidents.

2. Can I dye my hair if I have a shaved hairstyle?

Yes, you can dye your hair even if you have a shaved hairstyle. However, make sure that you go to a professional hairstylist who knows how to dye hair properly to avoid any damage to your hair.

3. How often should I get my shaved hairstyle trimmed?

You should get your shaved hairstyle trimmed every two to three weeks to maintain its shape and keep it looking neat and tidy.

4. Are shaved hairstyles suitable for all face shapes?

Shaved hairstyles can be customized to suit different face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best shaved hairstyle that suits your face shape.
