Brendon Urie Haircut: A Style Guide For 2023

Posted on November 25, 2022 by admin

Who is Brendon Urie?

Brendon Urie is an American singer-songwriter, musician, and multi-instrumentalist. He is the lead vocalist of the band Panic! at the Disco. Urie is known for his unique style and fashion sense, which includes his signature hairstyles.

What is the Brendon Urie haircut?

The Brendon Urie haircut is a short and spiky style that is textured with a messy finish. It is a versatile style that can be worn with or without bangs. The sides and back of the hair are usually shorter than the top, which is styled with a pomade or wax to create the textured look.

How to get the Brendon Urie haircut?

To get the Brendon Urie haircut, you need to have at least 2-3 inches of hair on top. Ask your hairstylist to cut the sides and back short, leaving the top longer. The hair on top should be textured with a razor or scissors to create a choppy look. Use a pomade or wax to style the hair, working it through with your fingers to create the desired messy finish.

What are the variations of the Brendon Urie haircut?

There are several variations of the Brendon Urie haircut, including the messy fringe, slicked-back, and undercut. The messy fringe is a longer version of the hairstyle with more volume and texture on the top. The slicked-back is a more formal style that is combed back with a pomade or gel. The undercut is a shorter version of the hairstyle with a shaved or buzzed side and back.

The Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a popular variation of the Brendon Urie haircut. It is a longer version of the hairstyle with more volume and texture on the top. To get this look, ask your hairstylist to cut the hair on top longer and add layers for more volume. Style the hair with a pomade or wax, working it through with your fingers to create the desired messy finish.

The Slicked-Back

The slicked-back is a more formal variation of the Brendon Urie haircut. It is combed back with a pomade or gel to create a sleek and polished look. To get this style, ask your hairstylist to cut the hair on top shorter and tapered at the sides and back. Use a comb to slick the hair back, adding more product as needed for hold.

The Undercut

The undercut is a shorter version of the Brendon Urie haircut. It features a shaved or buzzed side and back, with longer hair on top. To get this look, ask your hairstylist to shave or buzz the sides and back, leaving the top longer. Style the hair with a pomade or wax, working it through with your fingers to create the desired messy finish.

Tips for styling the Brendon Urie haircut

– Use a high-quality pomade or wax to style the hair. – Work the product through with your fingers for a natural and messy finish. – Experiment with different variations of the hairstyle to find the one that suits you best. – Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of the haircut.

Final thoughts

The Brendon Urie haircut is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that is popular among men in 2023. It is a versatile style that can be worn in different variations to suit your personal style and preferences. Whether you prefer a messy fringe, slicked-back, or undercut, the Brendon Urie haircut is a great choice for a modern and edgy look.
