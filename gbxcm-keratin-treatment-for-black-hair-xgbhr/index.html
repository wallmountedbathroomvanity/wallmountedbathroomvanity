Keratin Treatment For Black Hair: Everything You Need To Know In 2023

Posted on January 11, 2023 by admin

Introduction

If you are a black woman with relaxed hair, you know the struggle of maintaining healthy and shiny locks. One of the solutions that have been trending for years now is keratin treatment. It is a chemical process that smooths and straightens hair, making it easier to manage. In this article, we will be discussing everything you need to know about keratin treatment for black hair.

What is Keratin Treatment?

Keratin treatment is a chemical process that involves applying a protein called keratin to the hair. The keratin is then sealed into the hair cuticles using heat. The result is smooth, straight, and frizz-free hair that lasts for several months.

How Does Keratin Treatment Work?

Keratin treatment works by breaking down the hair’s disulfide bonds, which are responsible for the hair’s natural texture. The keratin is then applied to the hair, and the hair is sealed with a flat iron. The heat from the flat iron helps to lock in the keratin, resulting in smooth and straight hair.

Is Keratin Treatment Safe for Black Hair?

Yes, keratin treatment is safe for black hair. However, it is essential to note that some keratin treatments contain formaldehyde, which can be harmful to your health. It is recommended to use formaldehyde-free keratin treatments to avoid any health risks.

How Long Does Keratin Treatment Last?

Keratin treatment usually lasts for about three to six months, depending on how well you maintain your hair. To make it last longer, you should avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners and limit your use of hot tools.

Conclusion

Keratin treatment can be an excellent solution for black women with relaxed hair who want to achieve a sleek and manageable look. However, it is essential to choose a formaldehyde-free treatment and maintain your hair well to make it last longer. With the right care, you can enjoy smooth and shiny hair for several months.

FAQs

1. Can I color my hair after getting a keratin treatment?

Yes, you can color your hair after getting a keratin treatment. However, it is recommended to wait for at least two weeks before coloring your hair to allow the keratin to set in.

2. Can I wash my hair after getting a keratin treatment?

Yes, you can wash your hair after getting a keratin treatment. However, it is recommended to wait for at least three days before washing your hair to allow the keratin to set in.

3. Can I swim after getting a keratin treatment?

It is recommended to avoid swimming for at least a week after getting a keratin treatment. Chlorine and saltwater can damage the keratin and make it fade faster. If you have to swim, wear a swim cap or tie your hair up to avoid exposing it to the water.
